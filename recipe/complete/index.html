  • Gooey Butter Cake

    Difficulty:

    This gooey butter cake has a rich cake mix crust topped with a luscious baked cream cheese layer. It's super easy to make.


  • Prep Time: ~15 minutes

  • Cook Time: 45 minutes

  • Allergens: Milk, Wheat, Egg

  • Yield: 1 9x13-inch cake

  • Ingredients

    1 (18.25 ounce) package yellow cake mix
    Â½ cup butter, melted
    4 large eggs, divided
    2 teaspoons vanilla extract, divided
    1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese
    4 cups confectioners' sugar

    Equipment

    Cake pan (9x13)
    Electric mixer (optional)
    Mixing bowl

  • Directions

  • step 1:

    Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).

  • step 2:

    Mix together cake mix, butter, 2 eggs, and 1 teaspoon vanilla in a large bowl until well-combined. Pat mixture into a 9x13-inch dish.

  • step 3:

    Mix cream cheese, remaining 2 eggs, and remaining 1 teaspoon vanilla with an electric mixer in a bowl. Slowly beat in confectioner's sugar. Pour over cake layer.

  • step 4:

    Bake in the preheated oven until golden brown, 40 to 45 minutes. Cool before serving. Serve at room temperature and cover leftovers for up to a week.
